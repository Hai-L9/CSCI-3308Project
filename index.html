Worksite task coordination made simple

A corporate task tracker for managing work orders, assignments, and status updates across teams and contractors with a Kanban workflow and map-based planning.

View Project Overview

What this app does

Task Tracker helps organizations in field-operation industries manage tasks tied to real-world worksites. Managers and employees can create, assign, and track work items, while contractors only see the information relevant to their responsibilities.

Core Features

Authentication & Roles

Secure login/logout and role-based access for manager, employee, and contractor views.

Task & Kanban Management

Create tasks, assign owners, and move work through statuses in a board-style workflow.

Worksite + Maps

Associate tasks with worksites and use map location data for planning and visibility.

Target Industries

  • Telecommunications
  • Construction
  • Facilities management
  • Utility services
  • Disaster response / mitigation
  • Municipal and public works
